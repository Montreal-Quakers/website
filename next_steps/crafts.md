## Practical details
* This groups meets on after meeting the **first Sunday of the month** (every month except April and July 2026), from the end of meeting until 2:00 pm.
* People are welcome to bring projects they're working on. **No need to have any crafts**, as some activities will be planned.
* All are welcome!
